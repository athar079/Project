Welcome to CHANDRAYAAN

CHANDRAYAAN-A Science Rankers prepare the aspirants for JEE (IIT, NIT)/PRE-MEDICAL /+2 Board exams (11th /12th) & other state level competitive examinations as well as NDA Entrance Examination. CHANDRAYAAN provides best and quality education in very affordable fees. CHANDRAYAAN has shaped the dreams and careers of students from different parts of the nation since its existence.

At CHANDRAYAAN we work as mentor rather than just a teacher enable individuals to realize their potential and achieve their dreams. Our core ideology is to make student able to understand the nature of study they required and our focus on academic excellence, technological study innovations.

  • Hostel

    CHANDRAYAAN is fully residential with separate hostels for girls and boys. All students are provided accommodation on twin-sharing basis and all rooms are bath-attached.

  • Transport

    CHANDRAYAAN provides transport facility to receive as well as the drop at respective homes. With suitable vehicles for various routes, Keeping in safe the security for both boys and girls.

    The library provides information/knowledge resources through a carefully developed and balanced collection of Books, Journals, Magazines and Newspapers. The library is enabled with Wi-Fi and LAN facility for unlimited high speed internet access.
