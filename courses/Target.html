Target Engineering (RTE)

Aims & Objectives

›› Specially designed for the students aspiring for JEE.

›› Effectively focuses on JEE MAIN and JEE ADVANCED Prepration.

›› Plugging the loop holes in their subject knowledge.

›› Equips the students with all the skills and competencies required for their success in JEE MAIN and JEE ADVANCED.


Eligibility : Class 12th pass students

Duration : 1 year Classroom Program (Both class 11 + Class 12 Syllabus covered).

Course Description : Reboost is specially designed for students going to join the battle once again. The course effectively focuses on JEE-MAIN + ADVANCE preparation by plugging the loop holes in their subject knowledge and also equipping them with the skills for success.


Class Scheduling :

›› 5-6 days per week

›› 3 or 4 classes per day (any three/four of Physics, chemistry and Maths)


Class Timing :

›› For Boring Road - 8:00 AM-2:30 PM

›› For Kankarbagh - 8:00 AM-2:30 PM

›› For Bazar Samiti -7:30 AM-2:00 PM

›› Students may be called for more days and more classes during Holidays & Sundays at different timings.


Course Completion :

›› End of January 2019

›› After Course Completion : Revision Classes + Test Series (September/October 2018 till May 2019)
